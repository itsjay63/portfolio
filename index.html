Biography

I am a graduate student at the University of Maryland, College Park, and a Research Assistant at RAAS LAB. My current research focuses on image reconstruction for robotics navigation and reinforcement learning. I recently interned with Nokia Bell Labs for the summers, where I worked on object pose estimation.
